Policies
Daycare

The following is required for a dog to be eligible for Daycare:

  • Dog must have up to date Rabies, Bordatella and Distemper vaccinations
  • Dog must have a negative fecal exam from no more than one year ago
  • Dogs over one year old must have a negative heartworm blood test
  • Dogs over 9 months old must be fixed
  • Dog must be at least 4 months old and have had their Rabies, Bordatella and Distemper vaccinations for at least 14 days
  • Dog must pass a "Daycare Trial" as defined below
  • Owner must fill out a Daycare card and sign both sides
  • Owner must fill out a Boarding packet and sign each page

Dogs are seperated into Bigs and Littles based on size and temperment. Medium-sized dogs may be placed in either group depending where they are most comfortable

All groups of dogs are supervised by a kennel worker at all times

Bowls of water are placed in any space with dogs in it at all times
